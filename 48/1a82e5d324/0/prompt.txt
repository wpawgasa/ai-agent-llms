Here's a comprehensive list of conversation domains for call center services, organized by category:

---

## 🏢 Core Business Domains

### 1. Customer Account Management
- Account creation, verification, and closure
- Profile updates (address, contact info, email)
- Password reset and authentication
- Subscription management
- Loyalty/rewards program inquiries

### 2. Billing & Payments
- Invoice inquiries and disputes
- Payment processing and confirmation
- Refund and chargeback requests
- Payment plan negotiations
- Late fee waivers
- Tax and receipt requests

### 3. Order Management
- Order placement and tracking
- Order modification or cancellation
- Delivery status and rescheduling
- Return, exchange, and warranty claims
- Missing or damaged item reports

### 4. Technical Support
- Product setup and installation
- Troubleshooting and diagnostics
- Software/firmware update guidance
- Device compatibility questions
- Bug reporting
- Remote assistance escalation

### 5. Product & Service Information
- Product specifications and availability
- Pricing and promotions
- Feature comparisons
- Upgrade or upsell recommendations
- Service coverage and limitations

---

## 🏥 Industry-Specific Domains

### 6. Healthcare & Insurance
- Appointment scheduling and reminders
- Prescription refill requests
- Claims status and submission
- Coverage verification (benefits inquiry)
- Referral management
- Prior authorization

### 7. Banking & Financial Services
- Balance and transaction inquiries
- Fund transfers and wire requests
- Card activation, blocking, and replacement
- Loan and mortgage inquiries
- Fraud alerts and dispute resolution
- Interest rate and fee inquiries

### 8. Telecommunications
- Plan changes and upgrades
- SIM card and number porting
- Network outage reporting
- Roaming and international usage
- Device unlocking requests

### 9. Utilities (Electric, Water, Gas)
- Meter reading and billing disputes
- Service connection and disconnection
- Outage reporting and restoration ETA
- Energy usage analysis
- Green energy program enrollment

### 10. Travel & Hospitality
- Flight/hotel/car booking and modification
- Cancellation and rebooking
- Loyalty point redemption
- Check-in assistance
- Travel insurance claims
- Visa and documentation guidance

### 11. E-Commerce & Retail
- Product recommendations
- Stock availability and back-order status
- Coupon and voucher application
- Cart abandonment follow-up
- In-store vs. online price matching

### 12. Government & Public Services
- Tax filing assistance
- Permit and license inquiries
- Social benefit eligibility and status
- Document verification requests
- Complaint and grievance filing

## 🤝 Operational Domains

### 13. Complaints & Escalations
- Formal complaint registration
- Escalation routing to supervisors
- Service recovery and goodwill gestures
- SLA breach handling
- Follow-up and case closure

### 14. Appointment & Scheduling
- Booking service technicians
- Consultation scheduling
- Reminder confirmation and rescheduling
- Waitlist management

### 15. Sales & Lead Generation
- Inbound sales inquiries
- Outbound cold/warm calling
- Upsell and cross-sell conversations
- Promotional campaign responses
- Contract renewal negotiations

### 16. Surveys & Feedback
- Post-interaction CSAT surveys
- NPS collection
- Product/service feedback gathering
- Complaint trend reporting

### 17. Emergency & Critical Services
- Emergency dispatch coordination
- Critical incident reporting
- SOS and safety check-ins
- Service interruption notifications

## 🌐 Cross-Cutting Conversational Intents

Regardless of domain, every call center typically handles these **universal intents**:

| Intent | Description |
|---|---|
| **Greeting / Opening** | Initial contact and identity verification |
| **Clarification** | Re-asking or confirming customer intent |
| **Hold & Transfer** | Managing wait states and routing |
| **Authentication** | OTP, KYC, PIN verification |
| **Closing** | Summarizing resolution, follow-up promises |
| **Language Switch** | Handling multilingual requests |
| **Repeat / Rephrase** | Handling ASR misrecognition |
| **Out-of-scope** | Gracefully deflecting unknown topics |
| **Human Handoff** | Escalating to live agent |
